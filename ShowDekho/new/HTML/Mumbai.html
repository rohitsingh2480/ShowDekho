Mumbai Saga

Mumbai Saga: Directed by Sanjay Gupta.

2h 20m • Crime , Action • UA • Mar 19, 2021


About the movie

Mumbai Saga is the story of Amartya Rao who takes on the exploitative high and the mighty head-on, snatching his legitimate place under the sun. Mumbai Saga is the story of the duel between Amartya Rao and Senior Inspector Vijay Sawarkar and a story of hopes, aspirations, friendships, and betrayals in the backdrop of the early 90s. Most importantly, Mumbai Saga is the story of the departure of Bombay that was and the emergence of Mumbai that it became.
